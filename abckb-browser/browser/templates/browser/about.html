About

The Aliment to Bodily Condition knowledgebase highlights molecular mechanisms behind nutritive associations. This is accomplished through the connection of 10 disparate, siloed public resources and text mined associations from MEDLINE abstracts. This knowledgebase furthers precision nutrition hypotheses through the molecular mechanisms of plants on human health.
